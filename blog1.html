The Sound of Silence: A Vagabond's Heart

June 30, 2025

There’s a silence inside me louder than a scream.

I walk through crowds like a ghost with skin. People talk, laugh, live… but I float past, unseen. I’m not sad — not always — just alone in a world too loud to hear quiet souls like mine.

Silence is my shelter. Not because I chose it… but because the world chose noise. I speak to no one, and yet inside me, conversations roar. With myself. With my past. With the future I chase but never catch.

Some nights, I stare at the ceiling — like Musashi under the stars — and I wonder:
Am I lost? Or just becoming?

The truth is… even silence is a sound when it echoes long enough.
